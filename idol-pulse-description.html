Idol Pulse

📱 IdolPulse - The Ultimate K-Pop Stan Hub 🎶💜

🌟 App Summary

IdolPulse is an all-in-one K-pop fan hub designed to bring fans closer to their favorite idols. It provides real-time updates on concerts, music releases, voting events, and fan projects, while also fostering a vibrant community for discussions, streaming parties, and collaborations. The goal of IdolPulse is to unite K-pop fans worldwide, making it easier to stay updated, engage in fandom activities, and support idols through voting, streaming, and exclusive interactions.

🚀 Key Features of IdolPulse

📢 1. Fan Event Tracker & Concert Listings

Never miss an event! Stay updated with official concerts, fan meetings, streaming parties, and fan-organized projects in one place.

✅ Upcoming K-pop concerts & world tours

✅ Fan meet & greet schedules

✅ Streaming & voting party events

✅ Ticket links & reminders for sales

🗳️ 2. Voting & Streaming Support

Easily support your idols by staying informed about music show votes, fan polls, and streaming goals.

✅ Voting guides for M Countdown, MAMA, Melon, etc.

✅ Daily voting reminders & countdowns

✅ Streaming guides for Spotify, YouTube, and Melon

✅ Hashtags & fan campaign coordination

💬 3. K-Pop Community Hub

Connect with fellow stans and participate in fan discussions, collaborations, and group chats.

✅ Group chats by fandoms & artists

✅ Live discussions on comebacks & performances

✅ Fan-organized projects & fundraising for birthday ads, billboards, etc.

✅ Post & share fan art, edits, and theories

🔔 4. Push Notifications & Custom Alerts

Never miss an important update with real-time alerts for your favorite artists.

✅ Voting deadlines & ticket sales notifications

✅ Live stream & idol interaction alerts

✅ Concert countdowns & fan meeting schedules

💖 5. Idol Messaging & Subscription Guides

Get all the details on how to subscribe and chat with idols on official platforms like Weverse & Bubble.

✅ Subscription costs & exclusive content details

✅ Live chat notifications from idols

✅ Translation tools for international fans

🎟️ 6. Merch & Ticket Marketplace

Find and buy official & fan-made merchandise, including albums, photocards, and concert tickets.

✅ Official K-pop merchandise releases

✅ Fan resale ticket marketplace

✅ Verified fan-made merch (custom prints, lightstick accessories, etc.)

🌎 7. Multi-Language Support & Localization (Beta)

IdolPulse is designed for global K-pop fans, offering translation tools and multi-language content.

✅ Multi-language support (English, Korean, Spanish, Chinese, etc.)

✅ Auto-translation for official idol messages & posts

✅ Localized concert & voting schedules
